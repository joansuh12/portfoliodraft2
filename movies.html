Best Korean movies

Parasite...Minari...You might have already heard about these fabulous movies that were able to win prestigious awards and receive worldwide recognition for the first time in Korea. But there are also countless Korean movies that Koreans always keep in their minds as masterpieces. Check out this list of the best Korean movies of all time in addition to award-winning movies.

Award-winning Movies

Parasite

“Just being nominated along with fellow, amazing international filmmakers was a huge honor. I think we use only one language: the cinema.”
- Bong Joon Ho

Minari

“I’m sure it’s about time. Just hearing different stories, I think it’s very nice to understand each other. And we should embrace each other. People are categorised like black, white, yellow, brown … that is not a nice way to just divide like that, you know? I think we should put our colours together, make it prettier, even rainbows have seven colours. Colours don’t matter,”
- Youn Yuh Jung
